    Disruptive Innovation: North American Food Industry

    Current Market Cap: So far $2 Billion++ & Unicorn-in-the-Making

    Industry Movers: Innovations in Artifical Inteligence (AI), Robotics & Machine Learning

      Picture: (left) Julia Collins is the 2017 Women of Influence Award Winner, Silicon Valley star, 'Black Girls Code' influencer, GSB alum, Zume Pizza Co-founder & Co-CEO.
      Experience: Tried out all possible Pizza combinations and varieties from Zume's customized delivery & autobake oventruck right behind us in the picture above.
      Verdict: Loved the final product! I couldn't stop eating more pizza. Later that evening and over the next couple of weeks, repeatedly ordered from Zume just to get a feel for the typical consumer experience & thought their delivery service was pretty fast less bothersome than your normal pizza delivery.
      An really important take away from all the wonderful pizzas we ate for weeks was that they were super healthy, even for athletes on a strict diet. Which was absolutely good news for many millenial consumers looking for the right pizza without necessarily breaking that 'summer bod'(Some of my GSB & Mt. View friends went Zume Crazy because of this!). Julia explained that Zume was able to achieve this by investing more on sustainable and natural ingredients without neccesarily compromising on either margins or food quality. The Company had innovated more cost efficient ways to prepare, cook and deliver pizza for a fraction of the cost of its competitors. They could then plough back some of the margins to improve food supplies through quality control, data analytics and automation. Additionally, Zume had a robust consumer targeting & marketing strategy that relied on behavioural data aggregated from other industry players as well as themselves. This gave them a greater competitive edge when they used it to position their mobile cooking and delivery oventrucks in locations where they anticipated higher consumer demand, lowering processing times and increasing sales. For both new and loyal customers, Zume also offered an assortment of complimentary coupons and product offers that enabled them further customize their consumer order predictive processes and cut down on waste, thereby enhancing operational efficiency.
      Why Zume is such a big deal: Ordered Pizzas are made by Robots in single automated local food factory. These Pizzas are then delivered in autobake oventrucks to the consumer while still cooking. Zume is smartly sidestepping traditional production & overhead costs, intensitve human labor practices, franchises, stores & delivery inefficiencies; while still amazingly serving you affordable super fresh hot pizza. Now that's a real bargain!

    1. Role of Patronage in Broker Networks & Small Group Structures
      Understanding the Growth of Interpersonal Relationships & Opportunities within the 1st & 2nd Century Roman period
